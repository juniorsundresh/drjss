Welcome To Dr N Junior Sundresh's Portfolio Webpage


Located in the vibrant city of Chidambaram, Tamil Nadu, Government Cuddalore Medical College erstwhile Rajah Muthiah Medical College (RMMC) is a renowned institution for medical education in India. Established in 1985, the college is named after the late philanthropist Rajah Muthiah Chettiar, who had a vision to provide quality medical education to the youth of the region. The college is affiliated with Tamilnadu Dr MGR University and recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).
